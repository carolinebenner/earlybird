How It Works

  1. Upload a Document

    Upload any syllabus, course outline, or assignment document (PDF, Word, or text).

  2. Smart Date Extraction

    The system automatically scans for assignment due dates, exam dates, and other academic deadlines.

  3. Review & Edit

    Check the extracted dates, edit titles, and set specific times for your events before generating the calendar files.

  4. Download Calendar Files

    Get your .ics files, which are compatible with all major calendar applications including Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and Outlook.

  5. Import or Automatically Add

    Import the .ics files into your preferred calendar, or sign in with Google or Microsoft to add events directly to your calendar.
