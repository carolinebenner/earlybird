About Us

Our Mission

We're on a mission to simplify academic planning by eliminating the tedious task of manually entering deadline dates from syllabi into digital calendars.

Why We Built This

As former students, we experienced the beginning-of-semester rush to organize multiple course syllabi and track numerous deadlines across different classes. This tool was born from that experience.

Our goal is to help students, professors, and academic professionals save time and stay organized by automating the extraction of deadlines from course documents.

How It Works

Our application uses advanced text analysis and pattern recognition to identify assessment deadlines in academic documents. It's specifically designed to extract:

  • Assignment due dates
  • Quiz and exam schedules
  • Project deadlines
  • Presentation dates

Once extracted, these dates can be converted to calendar events for your preferred digital calendar.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. The documents you upload are processed temporarily and not permanently stored on our servers. Your information is never shared with third parties.

When you connect your Google or Microsoft account, we only request the minimum necessary permissions to add events to your calendar, and you can revoke these permissions at any time.
