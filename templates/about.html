Our Story

Early Bird was created by a team of Master of Management students at the Haskayne School of Business, University of Calgary. Inspired by the common challenge of managing multiple course deadlines and assessments, we set out to create a tool that would simplify academic life for students.

Our team recognized that the traditional method of manually entering assignment dates and deadlines into calendar apps was time-consuming and prone to errors. We wanted to leverage technology to automate this process, allowing students to focus more on learning and less on administrative tasks.

As part of our Entrepreneurial Thinking course (ENTI 674), we developed Early Bird from concept to implementation, combining our diverse backgrounds in technology, design, and business to create a practical solution for a real-world problem.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. The documents you upload are processed temporarily and not permanently stored on our servers. Your information is never shared with third parties.

When you connect your Google or Microsoft account, we only request the minimum necessary permissions to add events to your calendar, and you can revoke these permissions at any time.
